Customizing the Video and Playback Settings of a Portal Experience

{{ page.description }}

To configure the video and playback settings for a Portal Experience, edit the experience and click VIDEO AND PLAYBACK in the left navigation. The following settings are available:

  • Player and Lead Form - Select a player and Campaign lead form for the experience
  • Video - Configure video downloads, related links and description

Configuring player and lead form settings

The Player and Lead Form settings allow you to select a custom player for the experience and customize the player behavior. It's also possible to select a Campaign lead form and Campaign connection. For information on configuring the Player and Lead form settings, see Customizing the Player and Lead Form Settings for a Portal Experience.

Configuring video settings

The Video settings provide the ability to enable sorting, configure video downloads, display related links and select which video description (short or long) is displayed. For information on configuring the Video settings, see Customizing the Video Settings for a Portal Experience.
